# Manual Testing Queries for MFI Chatbot

Here is a comprehensive list of 20 queries designed to test all facets of the AI assistant, including general knowledge, live job listings, company policies, and edge cases. 

### General Company Information
1. What does Money Forward India do?
2. Can you tell me about the company's mission and vision?
3. What are the core values of the company?
4. What is the culture like at Money Forward India?
5. What technologies or tech stack does the company use?

### Live Job Listings & Careers
6. What jobs are currently open right now?
7. Do you have any Software Engineer or Developer roles available?
8. I'm looking for an internship. Are there any open positions?
9. What is the location for the Data Engineer role?
10. Can you provide the link to apply for the Backend Engineer role?
11. What is the salary for the Product Manager position?
12. Are there any remote jobs available?

### Policies & Data Security
13. How does Money Forward India handle my personal data?
14. Can you summarize your privacy policy?
15. What security practices and compliance measures does MFI follow?
16. Explain your data protection policy regarding third parties.

### Hiring Process
17. What is the hiring process like at Money Forward India?
18. How many interview rounds are there?

### Multi-part & Edge Cases
19. Can you give me a summary of the company's mission AND list the top 3 open jobs?
20. Can I order a pizza through you? *(Testing out-of-scope/fallback behavior)*
